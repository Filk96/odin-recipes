Guacamole

Description

This guacamole recipe gets a tasty kick from cayenne and cilantro. You can serve it smooth or chunky depending on your tastes.

Restaurant-worthy guacamole is just a few ingredients away.

You'll be surprised how easy it is to make this top-rated guacamole recipe. You'll come back to it again and again!

Ingredients

  • 3 avocados - peeled, pitted, and mashed
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 roma (plum) tomatoes, diced
  • Â½ cup diced onion
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 pinch ground cayenne pepper (Optional)

    • Steps

    1. Mash avocados, lime juice, and salt together in a medium bowl; mix in tomatoes, onion, cilantro, and garlic.

      Stir in cayenne pepper.
    2. Serve immediately, or cover and refrigerate for 1 hour for improved flavor.
